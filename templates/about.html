About the Philadelphia Pointing Span Test

Our Philadelphia Pointing Span Test (PPST) web application allows for the digital administration of assessments. The PPST digital system allows for medical professionals to administer tests and securely capture valuable patient data to support future evaluation and treatment. This application focuses on providing a user-friendly interface designed to improve accessibility, including for patients who may otherwise have difficulty completing the test. The PPST app is built with a strong emphasis on security, accessibility, and operational efficiency.

Accessibility Options

Our accessibility options include features designed to improve usability for all users, including those with disabilities. These include multiple languages and high contrast mode.

More on our Security

The PPST app is built with a strong emphasis on security. Patient data will not be tracked other than patient age.
