Test Instructions

What to Expect

  • On the following page, you will see a video explanation of how the test works.
  • After watching the video, you will have the opportunity to complete practice questions that will familiarize you with the test format.
  • These practice questions will not be counted towards your final results. They are just for practice.
  • Once you complete the practice section, the real test will begin.
  • During the test, you will need to pay close attention and press the buttons in the same order as the numbers or letters that appear on your screen.
  • Do your best to be as accurate and quick as possible during the real test portion.

Focus on accuracy first, then speed!
